About Shih Tzus

Shih Tzus are a small, sturdy breed known for their friendly and outgoing personalities. Originating from China, these dogs were bred to be companions and they excel at their job, often being described as "little lions". Shih Tzus are recognized by their long, flowing double coats and distinctive pushed-in faces.

Why We Love Shih Tzus

Shih Tzus are more than just pets; they're members of our family. Their loving and gentle nature makes them perfect companions. They're great with kids and other pets, and their alert and confident demeanor makes them excellent watchdogs. Despite their small size, Shih Tzus have a big personality and a charm that is irresistible.

Caring for Your Shih Tzu

Shih Tzus require regular grooming due to their long coats. Regular baths and daily brushing will keep their hair free from mats. They also need regular exercise to keep them healthy and happy. Despite their small size, Shih Tzus are quite active and enjoy short walks and playtime.
